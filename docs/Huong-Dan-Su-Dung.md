# Huong Dan Su Dung May Tinh

Tai lieu nay huong dan cach su dung may tinh voi trinh doc man hinh NVDA.

---

## Bat Dau

Khi may tinh bat len, NVDA se tu dong chay va noi "NVDA dang chay".

Sau do, ban o man hinh Desktop.

## Cac Cong Ket Noi

May tinh Dell Latitude 5420 co cac cong o hai ben va phia sau.

Dat may tinh truoc mat ban, man hinh mo len. Phia ban le o xa ban.

### Ben Trai

Tu phia sau ra phia truoc (tu ban le ve phia ban):

1. Cong USB-C nho (Thunderbolt) - dung de sac hoac ket noi man hinh ngoai
2. Cong USB-C nho thu hai (Thunderbolt)
3. Cong USB-A lon (hinh chu nhat) - cam USB cua ban vao day
4. Cong HDMI (hinh thang) - ket noi man hinh ngoai

### Ben Phai

Tu phia sau ra phia truoc (tu ban le ve phia ban):

1. Khe khoa chong trom (khe nho hinh tam giac)
2. Cong USB-A lon thu hai (hinh chu nhat) - cung cam duoc USB
3. Lo cam tai nghe (lo tron nho) - cam tai nghe vao day
4. Khe the microSD (khe mong)

### Phia Sau

Phia sau (gan ban le):

1. Cong mang RJ-45 (cong vuong lon) - ket noi mang day

### Tom Tat Nhanh

- Cam tai nghe: ben phai, lo tron, gan giua canh
- Cam USB: cong hinh chu nhat lon, mot cong moi ben (trai va phai)
- Sac may: cam day sac vao cong USB-C ben trai (cong gan ban le nhat)

## USB Cua Ban

Moi hoc sinh co mot USB rieng (16GB) voi so hieu (vi du: STU-001).

USB cua ban co the nhan bang chu noi Braille.

### Cam USB

1. Tim cong USB-A (hinh chu nhat lon) o ben trai hoac ben phai may tinh.
2. Dau cam USB chi vao duoc mot chieu. Neu khong vao, lat nguoc lai.
3. Day nhe nhang cho den khi nghe tieng "tach".

### Mo USB

1. Dung phim mui ten den bieu tuong "USB" tren Desktop (nhan phim "U" de nhay nhanh).
2. Nhan Enter. Cua so "This PC" se mo ra.
3. Nhan Tab de di chuyen vao danh sach o dia, dung phim mui ten de tim USB cua ban.
4. Nhan Enter de mo USB.

### Luu File Vao USB

Trong cac chuong trinh nhu Word:

1. Nhan phim F12 de mo cua so "Luu thanh" (Save As). Cua so nay tu dong tro den USB cua ban (o D).
2. Go ten file (vi du: baitap.docx).
3. Nhan Enter de luu.

Meo: Sau khi da luu file mot lan, nhan Ctrl + S se luu ngay tai vi tri cu — khong can hoi lai.

Neu USB khong duoc cam vao may, Word se luu file vao thu muc Documents tren may tinh. Cam USB roi luu lai bang F12 de chuyen file sang USB.

### Rut USB An Toan

Luon rut USB an toan de tranh mat du lieu.

1. Nhan phim Windows + E de mo File Explorer.
2. Nhan Tab den phan o dia, dung phim mui ten den USB cua ban.
3. Nhan phim Menu (hoac Shift + F10) de mo menu chuot phai.
4. Chon "Eject" va nhan Enter.
5. Doi cho den khi nghe thong bao an toan de rut USB.
6. Rut USB ra.

## Dieu Huong Tren Desktop

Desktop co cac bieu tuong xep theo thu tu ABC.

Dung phim mui ten (Trai, Phai, Len, Xuong) de di chuyen giua cac bieu tuong.

Nhan Enter de mo chuong trinh.

Meo: Nhan chu cai dau cua ten chuong trinh de nhay nhanh. Vi du, nhan "F" de nhay den Firefox, nhan "W" de nhay den Word.

Neu nhan chu cai khong co phan ung, tieu diem ban phim da roi khoi Desktop. Nhan phim Tab mot lan — NVDA se thong bao "Desktop, danh sach". Bay gio nhan chu cai de nhay se hoat dong. Neu Tab chuyen den thanh tac vu hoac khay he thong, nhan Tab them mot lan de quay lai danh sach Desktop. Hoac nhan mot phim mui ten bat ky de ep tieu diem len bieu tuong.

Giua cac lan nhay, nhan chu cai tiep theo trong vong khoang mot giay, hoac doi hai giay de bo dem "go chu" reset. Tam dung giua hai chu (vi du: "F" roi tam dung roi "W") co the khien Windows hieu la tien to "FW" va khong tim thay gi.

## Phim Tat NVDA Quan Trong

May tinh nay dung che do ban phim "Laptop". Phim NVDA co the la CapsLock hoac Insert (phim Insert ben phai hoac phim Insert tren bang so) — ca ba phim deu dung duoc nhu nhau.

| NVDA + L | Doc dong hien tai |

### Che Do Tap Trung

Khi dung PowerShell, Command Prompt, hoac cac ung dung khac ma ban phim khong hoat dong binh thuong:

Nhan Insert + Space de chuyen sang "Che do tap trung".

O che do nay, tat ca phim ban nhan se gui thang den ung dung (bao gom Backspace, Delete).

Nhan Insert + Space lan nua de quay lai che do duyet.

## Go Tieng Viet

UniKey tu dong chay khi bat may.

Mac dinh dung kieu go Telex.

Vi du Telex:
- aa = a (a mu)
- aw = a (a ngan)
- oo = o (o mu)
- ow = o (o moc)
- uw = u (u moc)
- dd = d (d ngang)
- s = dau sac, f = dau huyen, r = dau hoi, x = dau nga, j = dau nang
- z = xoa dau (vi du "af" cho ra "à", sau do them "z" tro lai "a")

Chuyen tieng Viet/Anh: nhan Ctrl + Shift. Xem bieu tuong UniKey tren thanh tac vu (V = tieng Viet, E = tieng Anh).

## Doc Sach Giao Khoa

May tinh co san 103 sach giao khoa tieng Viet (Canh Dieu, Chan Troi Sang Tao, Ket Noi Tri Thuc, Tieng Anh).

### Mo Readmate

1. Tren Desktop, nhan phim "R" de nhay den "Readmate".
2. Nhan Enter de mo chuong trinh.

### Chon Va Mo Sach

Khi Readmate mo, ban o mang hinh chinh voi tieu diem tai menu ben trai.

1. Nhan **Ctrl + B** de kich hoat tab Ke sach (neu dang o tab khac, Cai dat hoac Huong dan).
2. Nhan **F6** (hoac Tab) de di chuyen tieu diem sang khung sach.
3. Dung **phim mui ten** (Len/Xuong/Trai/Phai) de di chuyen qua danh sach sach — NVDA se doc ten tung sach.
4. Hoac nhan **Ctrl + F** de tim sach theo ten.
5. Khi tim duoc sach, nhan **Enter** de mo.

Sach se **tu dong bat dau doc** sau khi mo — khong can bam Ctrl+P. Bam Ctrl+P de tam dung hoac tiep tuc.

Luu y: **Phim Tab khong duyet qua tat ca sach** — chi dung phim mui ten hoac Ctrl+F.

### Doc Sach — Phim Tat Quan Trong

Khi sach da mo, dung cac phim sau:

| Phim | Chuc nang |
|---|---|
| Ctrl + P | Bat / tam dung doc |
| Mui ten Phai / Trai | Doan ke tiep / truoc |
| Ctrl + Mui ten Xuong / Len | Doan van ke tiep / truoc |
| Ctrl + Page Down / Page Up | Trang ke tiep / truoc |
| Alt + Mui ten Phai / Trai | Chuong ke tiep / truoc |
| H / Shift + H | Tieu de ke tiep / truoc |
| ] (ngoac vuong phai) | Doc nhanh hon |
| [ (ngoac vuong trai) | Doc cham hon |
| Ctrl + ] / Ctrl + [ | To am / giam am luong |
| Ctrl + F | Tim chu trong sach |
| Ctrl + N | Them ghi chu tai vi tri hien tai |
| Ctrl + Shift + N | Xem danh sach ghi chu |
| Ctrl + H | Danh sach tieu de (muc luc sach) |
| Ctrl + G | Danh sach trang |
| Ctrl + W | Dong sach va quay lai Ke sach |
| Esc | Quay lai mang hinh truoc |

### Dieu Huong Trong Readmate

- **Ctrl + Tab** hoac **F6**: chuyen giua cac khung (left sidebar, main content, v.v.)
- **Tab**: di chuyen trong mot khung (vi du: tu thanh tim kiem den danh sach sach)
- **Ctrl + B**: nhay thang den Ke sach
- **Ctrl + S**: Cai dat
- **Ctrl + H** (o mang hinh chinh, khong phai khi dang doc sach): Tab Huong dan cua Sao Mai

Neu NVDA im lang sau khi chuyen qua chuong trinh khac roi quay lai Readmate: nhan **Alt** roi **Esc** de kich hoat lai.

## Duyet Web Voi Firefox

### Hai Che Do Cua NVDA Tren Web

Khi ban dung Firefox, NVDA hoat dong o mot trong hai che do:

- **Che do Duyet (Browse)** — mac dinh tren trang web. NVDA tao ra "tai lieu ao" de ban di chuyen qua bang cac phim tat chu cai (H, K, F, v.v.).
- **Che do Tap Trung (Focus)** — tu dong bat khi ban bam vao o nhap van ban. Cac phim chu cai se go thay vi nhay.

Nhan **NVDA + Space** de chuyen thu cong giua hai che do. NVDA tu dong chuyen khi ban ra vao o nhap van ban.

### Phim Tat Duyet Web (Che Do Duyet)

| Phim | Chuc nang |
|---|---|
| Mui ten Xuong / Len | Doc dong ke tiep / truoc |
| H / Shift + H | Tieu de (heading) ke tiep / truoc |
| K / Shift + K | Lien ket (link) ke tiep / truoc |
| F / Shift + F | O nhap van ban ke tiep / truoc |
| B / Shift + B | Nut (button) ke tiep / truoc |
| D / Shift + D | Vung moc (landmark) — nhay qua menu, quang cao |
| 1 den 6 | Tieu de cap 1 den 6 |
| NVDA + F7 | Danh sach tat ca tieu de / lien ket / moc |
| NVDA + Mui ten Xuong | Doc lien tuc tu day (Say All) |
| Enter | Mo lien ket |

### Phim Tat Firefox

| Phim | Chuc nang |
|---|---|
| Ctrl + L | Nhay den thanh dia chi |
| Alt + Mui ten Trai | Quay lai trang truoc |
| Alt + Mui ten Phai | Tien den trang sau |
| Ctrl + T / Ctrl + W | Mo tab moi / Dong tab |
| Ctrl + Shift + T | Mo lai tab vua dong |
| Ctrl + F | Tim chu trong trang |
| F9 | Che Do Doc (Reader Mode) — bo quang cao, chi hien bai viet |
| F5 | Lam moi trang |
| Ctrl + + / Ctrl + - / Ctrl + 0 | Phong to / thu nho / tra lai mac dinh |

### Meo Doc Trang Tin Tuc (vnexpress.net, tuoitre.vn)

1. Mo trang tin → nhan **D** 2-3 lan de nhay qua menu va quang cao den vung "main" (noi dung chinh).
2. Nhan **H** mot lan → thuong nhay den tieu de bai viet.
3. Nhan **NVDA + Mui ten Xuong** → doc ca bai tu dau den cuoi.
4. Neu trang qua nhieu quang cao → nhan **F9** → Che Do Doc — chi con bai viet, xoa sach quang cao.
5. Nhan **F9** lan nua hoac **Alt + Mui ten Trai** de quay lai.

Quang cao tieng Anh se bi chan tu dong nho Firefox co uBlock Origin cai san. Neu van nghe quang cao xen vao, nhan **D** nhieu lan den khi NVDA noi "main" (noi dung chinh).

## Doc Bach Khoa Offline Voi Wikipedia

May tinh co Wikipedia tieng Viet offline (khong can mang).

1. Tu Desktop, nhan phim "W" de nhay den "Wikipedia" → Enter.
2. Nhan **F6** de nhay den thanh tim kiem.
3. Go chu de muon tim (vi du: "Vietnam") → nhan **Enter** de mo bai viet.
4. **Luu y quan trong**: neu NVDA khong doc bai viet tu dong, nhan **NVDA + Space** de bat Che Do Duyet. Do la su co da biet cua Kiwix khi dung voi NVDA.
5. Trong bai viet: dung **H** de nhay qua tieu de, **K** qua lien ket — giong nhu duyet web.
6. **Ctrl + F** de tim chu trong bai.
7. **Alt + Mui ten Trai** de quay lai ket qua tim kiem.

## Doc File PDF Voi Microsoft Edge

Khi ban mo file PDF (vi du: bai tap cua giao vien), may tinh se mo bang Microsoft Edge. Edge doc duoc noi dung PDF voi NVDA.

1. Mo file PDF (bam Enter tren file, hoac mo tu Word).
2. NVDA se chuyen sang che do duyet tu dong.
3. Dung **H** de nhay qua tieu de, **mui ten xuong** de doc dong theo dong.
4. Hoac nhan **Ctrl + Shift + U** trong Edge de bat "Doc To" (Read Aloud) — Edge se tu doc bai cho ban voi giong tieng Viet.
5. **Ctrl + F** de tim chu trong PDF.

Luu y: neu gap PDF duoc "quet hinh" (PDF hinh anh khong co chu) → NVDA se khong doc duoc. Phai nho giao vien cho ban file co chu.

2. Nhan **Ctrl + L** de nhay den o tim kiem.

4. Nhan **Alt + Page Down** hoac **Alt + Page Up** de chuyen giua nhom tu dien (Anh-Viet / Viet-Anh).
5. Nhan **Alt + Mui ten Xuong / Len** de xem ket qua tu tu dien khac cho cung mot tu.

## May Tinh Bo Tui (Calculator)

1. Mo Calculator tu Desktop (nhan phim "C" hoac dung phim mui ten).
2. Go phep tinh bang phim so va phim toan hoc: + - * / =
3. **Nhan Enter de nghe ket qua** — NVDA chi doc ket qua khi ban bam Enter, khong doc sau moi phim.
4. **Esc** hoac **Delete** de xoa va bat dau lai — NVDA se thong bao.

Vi du: go `25 + 17` roi nhan Enter → NVDA doc "42".

## Danh Van Dau Tieng Viet

Cac dau thanh trong tieng Viet (á, à, ả, ã, ạ) nghe kha giong nhau khi NVDA doc voi toc do nhanh.

De nghe ro dau thanh tren mot ky tu: dat con tro len chu cai → nhan **NVDA + dau cham (.) 2 lan nhanh**. NVDA se doc ky tu theo phien am va neu ten dau thanh.

Vi du: con tro tren "à" → NVDA doc "a co dau huyen".

De danh van ca mot tu: di chuyen qua tung chu bang phim mui ten, tai moi chu nhan NVDA + dau cham (.) 2 lan.

## Hoc Phim — Khi Ban Khong Biet Phim Nao La Gi

Khi ban cham tay len ban phim va khong chac phim do lam gi, co hai cach de hoc ma khong lam gi sai:

### Cach 1: Che Do Hoc Phim Cua NVDA (NVDA + 1)

Bat cu luc nao, bat cu chuong trinh nao:

1. Nhan **NVDA + 1** de bat "Che do hoc phim". NVDA noi "Input help on".
2. Bay gio nhan bat ky phim nao → NVDA doc ten phim va chuc nang, **khong thuc thi phim do**. An toan tuyet doi.
3. Ban co the tham do toan bo ban phim ma khong so gay loi.
4. Nhan **NVDA + 1** lan nua de tat che do (phim hoat dong binh thuong tro lai).

Day la cach nhanh nhat de nho lai mot phim.

### Cach 2: Sao Mai Typing Tutor (SMTT)

Chuong trinh day go phim cua Sao Mai, da duoc cai san tren may:

1. Tren Desktop, nhan phim "S" de nhay den "Sao Mai Typing Tutor" → Enter.
2. Chon "Kham pha ban phim" (Explore Keyboard).
3. Nhan bat ky phim nao → SMTT doc ten phim bang tieng Viet.
4. SMTT con co bai hoc go phim co cau truc de luyen hang ngay — dac biet huu ich cho Telex.

**So sanh**: NVDA+1 dung nhanh o bat ky dau; SMTT tot cho hoc co ban co cau truc.

### Cach Dinh Huong Tren Ban Phim (Dell Latitude 5420)

- **F** va **J** co not noi san — day la vi tri "hang chinh" (home row) cho 8 ngon tay.
- **Delete** va **Mui ten Xuong** cung co not noi tu nha san xuat.
- **CapsLock** hoac **Insert** deu co the dung lam phim NVDA. CapsLock nam ben trai ban phim, cach phim Shift trai hai hang phia tren. Insert nam ngay canh Delete — co not noi nho them vao.
- Tu F/J, dem ra cac phim khac: ben trai F la D → S → A; ben phai J la K → L → dau cham phay.

## Giu An Toan Phan Cung

- Dong man hinh bang hai tay, giu hai goc ngoai de khoi hong ban le.
- Cac bieu tuong dinh vi: trackpad = phia truoc, ban le = phia sau.
- Cam day sac vao cong **USB-C ben trai, gan ban le nhat**.
- Khong an uong gan may. Ban phim chong tran nhung khong chong nuoc tran.
- Rut tai nghe bang cach cam dau cap, khong keo day.
- Neu NVDA bat ngo im lang, nhan **NVDA + Shift + B** de nghe trang thai pin — co the pin sap het.
- **Khong** bat BitLocker, khong doi mat khau, khong dang nhap tai khoan Microsoft — goi thay co truoc.
