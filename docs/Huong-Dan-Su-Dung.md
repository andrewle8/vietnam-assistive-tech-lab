## USB Cua Ban

Moi hoc sinh co mot USB ca nhan (16GB) voi so hieu (vi du: STU-001).

USB cua ban co nhan chu noi Braille gan kem.

### Cam USB

1. Tim cong USB-A (hinh chu nhat lon) o ben phai may tinh, gan lo cam tai nghe.
2. Dau cam USB chi vao duoc mot chieu. Nhan Braille phai huong len tren.

### Mo USB

1. Dung phim mui ten den bieu tuong "USB" tren Desktop (nhan phim "U" de nhay nhanh).
2. Nhan Enter. Cua so "This PC" se mo ra.
3. Vi tri luu se chi ve USB cua ban.

### Luu Tap Tin Vao USB

Luu tat ca cac tai lieu quan trong vao USB cua hoc sinh.

Trong cac chuong trinh nhu Word:

1. Nhan F12 de mo hop thoai "Save As" (Luu Duoi Dang). Hop thoai tu dong chi vao USB cua hoc sinh.
2. Chon thu muc luu tren USB.
3. Go ten tap tin (vi du: baitap.docx).
4. Nhan Enter de luu.

Sau lan luu dau tien, Ctrl + S se luu vao cung vi tri ma khong hoi.

Neu USB chua duoc cam, Word se luu vao thu muc Documents tren may tinh. Cam USB vao va nhan F12 de luu lai vao USB.

## Tu The Dat Tay Khi Go Phim

Tu the dat tay nay giup cac ngon tay tim va ghi nho vi tri tung phim.

- Dung hai ngon tro tim phim F va J. Phim F va J co mot go nho o duoi mat phim va nam gan giua ban phim, o hang phim thu ba tu duoi len.
	- F va J nam o "hang phim chinh" (home row) — noi tat ca cac ngon tay (tru ngon cai) dat nghi.
	- Dat cac ngon con lai len cac phim ke ben. Tu F/J, dem sang cac phim khac: ben trai F la D, S, A; ben phai J la K, L va dau cham phay (;).
	- Ben phai F la G. Ben trai J la H.
- Ca hai ngon cai co the dat ho hoac nghi tren thanh dai (phim lon hinh chu nhat o giua hang phim duoi cung — hai hang duoi hang F/J).

## Bo Cuc Ban Phim

- Cac phim mui ten o goc duoi ben phai ban phim.
- Tim phim Mui ten xuong — phim nay co go nho giong F va J. Phia tren la Mui ten len, hai ben la Mui ten trai va Mui ten phai.
- Tu thanh dai (Spacebar), dem sang phai: Alt phai, Control, roi den cac phim mui ten.
- Phim Delete va Mui ten xuong cung co go nho o mep duoi phim.
- **CapsLock** hoac **Insert** deu co the dung lam phim NVDA. CapsLock la phim ngoai cung ben trai cua hang phim chinh (hang A/S/D/F), ngay phia tren phim Shift trai. Insert o ngay canh phim Delete, ben phai ban phim.

## Hoc Phim

Neu ngon tay ban lo cham vao mot phim va ban khong biet do la phim gi, co hai cach an toan de tim hieu:

### Cach 1: Che do Tro giup Phim cua NVDA (NVDA + 1)

1. Nhan **NVDA + 1** de bat "Che do Tro giup Phim". NVDA thong bao "Input help on (vietnamese)".
2. Bay gio nhan bat ky phim nao — NVDA se doc ten va chuc nang cua phim do ma khong thuc thi phim. Ban co the thu moi phim tren ban phim.
3. Nhan **NVDA + 1** lan nua de thoat (cac phim tro lai hoat dong binh thuong).

Day la cach nhanh nhat de goi nho mot phim.

### Cach 2: Sao Mai Typing Tutor (SMTT)

Phan mem luyen go phim tieng Viet cua Sao Mai:

1. Tren Desktop, nhan "S" de nhay den "Sao Mai Typing Tutor" → Enter.
2. Chon "Kham pha ban phim" (Explore Keyboard).
3. Nhan bat ky phim nao → SMTT se doc ten phim bang tieng Viet.
4. SMTT cung co cac bai luyen go hang ngay — dac biet huu ich cho ca ban phim tieng Viet va tieng Anh.

**So sanh**: NVDA+1 phu hop khi can tra phim nhanh o bat cu dau; SMTT phu hop de luyen tap co he thong.

## Go Tieng Viet

May tinh dung UniKey va ban phim mac dinh la tieng Viet.

De chuyen giua Tieng Viet va Tieng Anh: nhan Ctrl + Shift.

Phuong phap go mac dinh la Telex.

Vi du Telex (chu ghep — moi dong co ghi chu trong ngoac de ban khong dau van hieu duoc):

- aa = a (a co dau mu / a-circumflex)
- aw = a (a co dau trang / a-breve)
- ee = e (e co dau mu / e-circumflex)
- oo = o (o co dau mu / o-circumflex)
- ow = o (o co dau moc / o-horn)
- uw = u (u co dau moc / u-horn)
- dd = d (d co gach ngang / d-stroke)

Vi du Telex (dau thanh):

- s = dau sac (acute) — vi du a, e, i, o, u, y
- f = dau huyen (grave) — vi du a, e, i, o, u, y
- r = dau hoi (hook above) — vi du a, e, i, o, u, y
- x = dau nga (tilde) — vi du a, e, i, o, u, y
- j = dau nang (dot below) — vi du a, e, i, o, u, y
- z = bo dau (vi du go "af" cho ra a, sau do "z" cho ra a)

Vi du ket hop:

- aas = a (a voi dau sac / a-circumflex with acute)
- aaf = a (a voi dau huyen / a-circumflex with grave)
- aar = a (a voi dau hoi / a-circumflex with hook)
- aax = a (a voi dau nga / a-circumflex with tilde)
- aaj = a (a voi dau nang / a-circumflex with dot)
- aws/awf/awr/awx/awj cho a (a-breve) voi nam dau thanh
- ees/eef/eer/eex/eej cho e (e-circumflex) voi nam dau thanh
- oos/oof/oor/oox/ooj cho o (o-circumflex) voi nam dau thanh
- ows/owf/owr/owx/owj cho o (o-horn) voi nam dau thanh
- uws/uwf/uwr/uwx/uwj cho u (u-horn) voi nam dau thanh
- DD = D (d-stroke chu hoa), dd = d (d-stroke chu thuong)
- Vieejt = Viet
- ngwowif = nguoi
- ddungs = dung
- caarm own = cam on

Chuyen Viet/Anh: nhan Ctrl + Shift.

## Nghe Dau Thanh Tieng Viet

De nghe dau thanh tren mot ky tu: di chuyen con tro den chu cai → nhan **NVDA + dau cham (.) hai lan lien tiep**. NVDA se doc ky tu theo cach phat am va noi ten dau thanh.

Vi du: con tro o chu "a" (go Telex la "af", a co dau huyen) → NVDA doc "a with grave accent".

De doc ca tu, di chuyen tung chu cai bang phim mui ten, moi chu nhan NVDA + dau cham (.) hai lan.

## Doc Sach Giao Khoa

May tinh da cai san 103 cuon sach giao khoa tieng Viet (Canh Dieu, Chan Troi Sang Tao, Ket Noi Tri Thuc, Tieng Anh).

### Mo Readmate

1. Tren Desktop, nhan "R" de nhay den "Readmate".
2. Nhan Enter de mo.

### Chon Va Mo Sach

Khi Readmate mo, ban o man hinh chinh voi tieu diem tren thanh ben trai.

1. Nhan **Ctrl + B** de mo tab Bookshelf (Ke Sach) — neu dang o Settings hoac User Guide.
2. Nhan **F6** (hoac Tab) de chuyen tieu diem sang khung danh sach sach.
3. Dung **phim mui ten** (Len/Xuong/Trai/Phai) de duyet qua danh sach — NVDA se doc ten tung cuon sach.
4. Hoac nhan **Ctrl + F** de tim sach theo ten.
5. Khi tim thay cuon muon doc, nhan **Enter** de mo.

Luu y: **Tab khong duyet qua het danh sach sach**. Hay dung phim mui ten hoac Ctrl+F.

NVDA se doc tieu de sach va tien do doc. Nhan Control neu muon dung giong doc.

Sau do, khi da san sang doc sach, nhan **Ctrl + P** de bat dau nghe sach.

## Duyet Web Voi Firefox

### Hai Che Do Cua NVDA Tren Web

Khi dung Firefox, NVDA hoat dong o mot trong hai che do:

- **Che do Duyet (Browse Mode)**: che do mac dinh tren trang web. NVDA tao ra mot "tai lieu ao" de ban duyet bang cac phim tat mot chu cai (H, K, F, v.v.).
- **Che do Tieu diem (Focus Mode)**: tu dong bat khi ban vao o nhap van ban. Phim chu cai se go binh thuong thay vi nhay.

Nhan **NVDA + Space** de chuyen giua hai che do. NVDA tu doi che do khi vao/roi o nhap van ban.

5. Nhan **F9** lan nua hoac **Alt + Mui ten trai** de quay lai.

## Wikipedia Va Wiktionary Offline

May tinh co Wikipedia va Wiktionary tieng Viet offline (khong can mang). Ca hai deu mo trong Firefox de cac phim tat che do Duyet cua NVDA hoat dong binh thuong.

### Cach Nhanh Nhat: Tim Trong Ca Hai Cuon Sach Cung Luc

1. Tren Desktop, nhan **W** de nhay den "Wikipedia" → nhan **Enter**. Firefox se mo trang chu Kiwix (liet ke hai cuon: Wikipedia va Wiktionary).
2. Nhan **Tab** 3 lan — tieu diem se vao o tim kiem chung (tim ca Wikipedia va Wiktionary).
3. Go chu de muon tim (vi du: "Viet Nam") → nhan **Enter**. Mot danh sach ket qua se hien ra voi cac muc tu ca hai cuon sach.
4. Nhan **Mui ten xuong** de duyet qua cac ket qua, roi nhan **Enter** vao ket qua muon doc.

### Tim Rieng Trong Wikipedia (Hoac Wiktionary)

1. Tu trang chu Kiwix, nhan **Tab** 5 lan de den o **Wikipedia**. (Them mot Tab nua — tong cong 6 — se den o **Wiktionary**.) Nhan **Enter** de mo cuon sach; Trang Chinh (doi voi Wikipedia) se hien ra.
2. Nhan **Tab** mot lan — tieu diem se vao o tim kiem rieng cua cuon sach do.
3. Go chu de → nhan **Enter**. Cac ket qua chi tu cuon sach do se hien ra.
4. Nhan **Mui ten xuong** de duyet qua cac ket qua, **Enter** tren mot ket qua de mo bai viet.

### Trong Bai Viet

- NVDA se tu doc bai viet.
- **H** / **Shift + H** — tieu de ke tiep / tieu de truoc
- **K** / **Shift + K** — lien ket ke tiep / lien ket truoc
- **Mui ten xuong** — doc dong theo dong
- **Ctrl + F** — tim chu trong bai hien tai (chuc nang Tim tren trang cua Firefox; co the bat "Match Diacritics" de tim chinh xac theo dau thanh)
- **Alt + Mui ten Trai** — quay lai danh sach ket qua (hoac quay xa hon ve trang chu Kiwix)

## Doc File PDF Voi Microsoft Edge

Khi ban mo file PDF, may tinh se mo bang Microsoft Edge. NVDA doc file PDF tot nhat qua Edge.

1. Mo file PDF (bam Enter tren file, hoac mo tu Word).
2. NVDA se tu chuyen sang che do Duyet.
3. Dung **H** de nhay qua tieu de, **mui ten xuong** de doc dong theo dong.
4. Hoac nhan **Ctrl + Shift + U** trong Edge de bat "Doc Thanh Tieng" (Read Aloud) — Edge se doc tai lieu cho ban bang giong tieng Viet.
5. **Ctrl + F** de tim chu trong PDF.
